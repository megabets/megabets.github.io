      • In the 🏆 Podium tab, predict the tournament's final standings. Points are awarded only for an exact placement match. Picks lock on 4 July (end of the Round of 32).
      Other rules
      • Place your bet on each match before that match kicks off — you can update it until kickoff. Once a match starts, your bet for it is locked.
      • If you don't place a bet before kickoff, you score 0 points for that match.
      • On the leaderboard, ties are broken by most exact-score hits, then by most correct outcomes.
      • Knockout matches are settled on the score after 90 minutes (regulation). Extra time and penalty shootouts don't count toward your bet.
      • If a match is postponed or cancelled, bets on it carry over to its rescheduled date.
      Prize money
      • Everyone pays €20 to enter — the whole pot (players × €20) goes back out as prizes.
      • Depending on how many participants there are, we'll likely split the money among top 5.
